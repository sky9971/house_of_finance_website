Frequently Asked Questions?

What is Robo advisory?

Robo-advisors are digital/online platforms that provide automated, algorithm-driven financial planning and investing services with little to no human supervision.(This definition is from Investopedia)

It is a feature of Robo advisory which makes the investment on behalf of you in the recommended portfolio.
